#prac-4b Automate any process using desktop recording.

Writing and Calculation operation using desktop recording
Step 1: Open UiPath studio. select process and give name to your project.
Step 2: Click on open main workflow. Click on new select flowchart.
Step 3 : Open recording and click on desktop recording
Step 4 : Open notepad and calculator
Step 5: click on recording and start to record.
Step 6: Type message on the calculator.
Step 7: Perform a simple calculation in the calculator.
Step 8: Save and exist the recorder.
Step 9: Run the file
